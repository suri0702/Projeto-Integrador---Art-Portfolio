Commissioned works are always welcome and truly appreciated. I love collaborating with clients to bring their unique visions to life through art. Whether you’re looking for a personalized piece of Zodiac art, a custom Hamsa, a special watercolor creation, or a beautifully designed Ketubah, I take pride in crafting artwork that reflects your individual style, story, and emotions. Every commissioned piece is a unique journey, and I’m excited to explore new ideas and bring your concepts into reality.


If you're interested in a custom piece, feel free to reach out via social media, email or the contact page. I’m always happy to discuss your ideas and work together to create something special just for you.
