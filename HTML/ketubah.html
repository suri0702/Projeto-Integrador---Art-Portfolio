A Ketubah is much more than a traditional marriage contract—it is a cherished work of art that celebrates the bond between two people. Rich in history and meaning, the Ketubah outlines the promises and values of the couple, becoming a lasting symbol of their commitment. My Ketubah designs range from deeply traditional styles with intricate, ornate details, to modern interpretations that reflect a contemporary aesthetic. Each Ketubah I create is crafted with care, ensuring it reflects the uniqueness of your special day and your love story.

Whether you're drawn to a more classic design or something with a fresh, modern feel, I offer a variety of Ketubah options to suit your personal taste and wedding theme. My goal is to create a piece that is not only beautiful but also meaningful—a keepsake that will be treasured for years to come.

If you’re looking for something truly one-of-a-kind, I also offer custom Ketubahs, allowing you to add personal touches that reflect your story, values, and vision. Visit the Commissioned Work section to collaborate on a bespoke Ketubah, and together we’ll design a piece that perfectly captures the spirit of your wedding.
