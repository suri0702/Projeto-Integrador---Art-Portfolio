About

Hello

I'm Simony Artsy, a Brazilian artist based in London. I explore various forms of art, blending creativity and culture in every piece I create.

My work is inspired by the beauty around me and reflects a deep connection to nature, vibrant colors, and meaningful stories. From watercolor paintings to custom artwork, I strive to evoke emotion and capture moments in a way that feels personal and unique.

Whether it's a serene landscape, a delicate floral composition, or a bespoke creation for a special occasion, my art aims to bring a sense of joy and tranquility. Based in London, but with roots in Brazil, my artistic journey merges diverse influences, giving each piece a touch of both tropical warmth and European elegance.

Thank you for visiting my page, and I hope my work speaks to you as much as it does to me!
