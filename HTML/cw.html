Commissioned works are always welcome and truly appreciated. I love collaborating with clients to bring their unique visions to life through art. Whether you’re looking for a personalized piece of Zodiac art, a custom Hamsa, a special watercolor creation, or a beautifully designed Ketubah, I take pride in crafting artwork that reflects your individual style, story, and emotions.
